## About the Role

We are seeking a dedicated Project Coordinator to lead our sustainable agriculture initiatives in the Makawanpur district. You will work closely with local farmers, community groups, and our technical experts to implement modern, eco-friendly farming practices.

## Key Responsibilities

- Plan and oversee the implementation of model farming projects.
- Coordinate capacity-building training sessions for marginalized farmers.
- Monitor project progress and prepare regular impact reports.
- Liaise with local government bodies and stakeholders.

## Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Agriculture, Rural Development, or a related field (Master's preferred).
- At least 3 years of field experience in community-based agricultural projects.
- Strong communication and reporting skills in both Nepali and English.
- Willingness to travel extensively to rural project sites.

## What We Offer

- A supportive and inclusive team environment.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth and training.
- The chance to make a tangible difference in the lives of rural communities.
